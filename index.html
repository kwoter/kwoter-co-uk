02The journey

So... how does it work?

  1. 01
    01

    Lead Integration

    Leads are automatically populated into the Kwoter system, either from your existing lead sources or from details entered during a client initiated call.

  2. 02
    02

    Broker Verifies Details

    Brokers ensure accuracy and compliance by verifying customer information, addressing all necessary GDPR-related queries.

  3. 03
    03

    Form Completion

    The broker fills out the Kwoter form, including all essential questions to enable the display of quotes from various insurers.

  4. 04
    04

    Broker Clicks 'Get Quotes'

    After inputting the required information, the broker clicks 'Get Quotes'. Kwoter then starts processing the data to generate quotes.

  5. 05
    05

    Instant Quote Display

    With a single click, prices from all insurers are displayed on one screen, streamlining the decision-making process and enhancing client satisfaction.

  6. 06
    06

    Sale Completion

    Once the sale is concluded, the transaction details are automatically integrated into your CRM system, facilitating the policy binding and the issuance of necessary documents.

07Kwoter AI

Meet your AI workforce

We're building agentic AI directly into Kwoter: named agents that answer, dial, coach and research alongside your team, around the clock. Every one remembers, learns from each conversation, and can be trained on your own recorded calls. Ten are planned. Meet the first six.

Explore Kwoter AI→

Roxanne

The receptionist

01 / 10

The receptionist who never goes home. When your team clocks off at six, Roxanne answers every call with a real conversation, not a voicemail.

  • Handles every out-of-hours call, 24 hours a day
  • Joins the hunt group as your 11th, 12th or 50th agent when lines are full
  • Passes messages to claims, renewals or any desk by email, WhatsApp, text or internal note
  • Takes full applications over the phoneSoon

Otto

The outbound caller

02 / 10

Your book, worked around the clock. Otto dials lapsed leads and upcoming renewals, has the conversation, and hands anyone ready to buy straight to a human.

  • Re-solicits last year's leads as their renewal dates come round
  • Talks clients through renewal quotes and confirms acceptance on the call
  • Fills out applications and transfers hot prospects to your team
  • Scales without hiring: a ten-person brokerage that calls like fifty

Max

The marketing analyst

03 / 10

Plugged into Google, Meta and TikTok through their APIs, Max reads every advert's performance and tells you each morning what to scale and what to switch off.

  • Daily, weekly, monthly and yearly reports, broken down by product line
  • Spots ad fatigue and rising cost per lead before they hurt
  • Designs and runs your email campaigns, and plans call campaigns for Otto
  • Drafts replacement ads ready for one-click human approvalSoon

Sage

The sales coach

04 / 10

On every call your humans take, Sage scores compliance, coaches in the moment, and turns your best seller's calls into training for the rest.

  • Scores every call against your compliance checklist and flags management when needed
  • Live coaching on sales technique, compliance and etiquette
  • Lifts winning moments from your top calls into training for the team
  • Surfaces off-script promises before they become an FCA problem

Cleo

The claims handler

05 / 10

The calm voice at 2am when a client has just had a crash. Cleo knows the entire claims process and gets people to exactly what they need, fast.

  • Trained across vehicle, motorbike and home claims, end to end
  • Arranges recovery, hire cars and the right third-party services on the spot
  • Tells clients exactly what details to take at the scene
  • Escalates to your human team, or straight to 999 when it's serious

Intel

Intel & research

06 / 10

Your eyes on the whole market. Brief Intel with a question and he comes back with what your competitors are doing and what it means for you.

  • Tracks competitor moves, products and pricing angles
  • Studies long-running winning adverts and rebuilds the angle in your words
  • On-demand research across compliance, financial and market questions
  • Point him at anything: he reports back with what you need
